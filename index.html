The idea for the MPC3000AD is built on the famous sampler machines produced by Akai in the late 1980s. The original Akai MPC 60 and 3000 models were designed to playback audio samples when a trigger pad had been pressed.

The MPC3000AD follows the mechanism of those original MPCs but reverses the time frame - rather than playing back recorded sounds of the past, the MPC3000AD samples audio from the future.

The scenario on this page is based on Plagues Futures a creative project led by Dr Caitlin McDonald exploring future plagues with scenarios encompassing volcanic eruptions, environmental breakdown and media authoritarianism.
